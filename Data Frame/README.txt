
***Labels used in the column names and their meanings****


syl: Number of syllables

l_rhymes: Number of link rhymes

int_rhymes: Number of internal rhymes

unique_w: Number of unique words

lps: Link rhymes per syllable

ips: Internal rhymes per syllable

lpl: Link rhymes per line

ipl: Internal rhymes per line

lpw: Link rhymes per word

ipw: Internal rhymes per word

ipuw: Internal rhymes per unique word

lpuw: Link rhymes per unique word
